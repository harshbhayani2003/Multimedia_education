Game Design Development Course

Game design is a large field, drawing from computer science/programming, creative writing, and graphic design. Game designers take the creative lead in imagining and bringing to life video game worlds. Game Development is the art of creating games and describing a game’s design, development and release. It may involve concept generation, design, build, testing and release. While creating a game, it is important to consider the game mechanics, rewards, player engagement and level design.
