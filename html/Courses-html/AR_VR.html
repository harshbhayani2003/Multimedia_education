About AR-VR Development Course in Surat

Discover the realistic worlds of augmented reality and virtual reality development. Your ability to blend skills in technology with artistic vision produces captivating, enjoyable interactions. The entire course has been developed with the aid of the flexible Unity engine to provide aspiring developers all the skills and knowledge they require to produce creative AR/VR apps.

The amazing, hands-on AR-VR Development with Unity course reveals beginners to the world of AR and VR technology and shows them how to utilize it to create realistic virtual worlds. You can also learn Unity 3D Course in Surat.

Course Duration 6 Months

Daily Time 2 Hours

Eligibility For This Course

✓ knowledge in C, C++, C#, and Unity

Included In This Course

✓ Job Support

✓ Rich Learning Content

✓ Taught by Experienced Prof.

✓ Industry Oriented Projects

Course Modulers

Introduction to Augmented Reality (AR), Virtual Reality (VR)

  • 🞉 AR Module:
  • 🞄 Introductory Overview of AR Technologies
  • 🞄 AR System Configuration
  • 🞄 Basic Face Filter Design
  • 🞄 Building an AR Application Using Unity
  • 🞄 Development of a Plane Detection AR App
  • 🞄 Implementing Single Image Detection
  • 🞄 Techniques for Multiple Image Detection
  • 🞄 Exploring Occlusion in AR
  • 🞄 Understanding and Implementing Markerless Tracking
  • 🞄 Incorporating Physics Simulations into Markerless Environments
  • 🞄 Managing the Visibility of Augmented Objects
  • 🞄 Future Directions and Advanced Themes in AR

  • 🞄 Introductory Overview of VR Technologies

  • 🞄 Importing Oculus Integration and Game Assets with Unity or Unreal Engine

  • 🞄 Direct Touch and User Interface Interaction
