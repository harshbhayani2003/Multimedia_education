6 Months
Video Editing Course

In this 6-month course, you'll explore the realms of video editing and graphic design to master the art of storytelling through multimedia. Starting with Adobe Premiere Pro, you'll learn to craft seamless narratives by cutting and enhancing video footage. Dive into Adobe After Effects for breathtaking visual effects and motion graphics. Additionally, you'll delve into audio editing using software like Adobe Audition to enhance sound quality and create immersive experiences. You'll also discover the power of Adobe Photoshop for graphic design and asset creation.
