About the Program Leader and Trainer

Mohit Rajput is a professional movie poster designer and behind creating the Official Posters of some of the most famous web series and movies like Mumbai Saga, Mirzapur, The Family Man, Scam 1992, Pataal Lok, Bhonsle, Pagglait and many more.

In just a few years, he became a successful professional celebrity photo & movie poster designer. He is also a professional Photoshop mentor and works relentlessly towards providing a platform for young photo enthusiasts to learn and enhance their skill sets more than 750+ students trained and coached by him are now full-time professionals and fulfilling their aspirations!

Program Overview

Learn graphic design, photo editing & photo retouching in Photoshop
