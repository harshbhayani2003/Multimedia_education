Why do students not get jobs even after college or higher education?

Because even though the school or college has good teachers and excellent staff, the syllabus taught is very old. Also, according to the industry or market, the latest knowledge which should be in the student is not available. As a result, the students really lack the skills and abilities due to which it is difficult to get a job immediately after completing college or not getting a job based on the field of education they have taken.

There are two reasons for that

Lack of skill based on education in school and college

Choice of course with the interest of inner strength and skill of students

The Red & White Group of Institutes has been working for the last 15 years with the same objective in mind.


"Each Indian youth can acquire practical, business and job-oriented education in today's fast growing sector according to his/her skills and build an international career."


Red & White provides education with practical as well as skill-based knowledge and working as a Bridge between the existing education system and the industry.Therefore, the course for studying in the field in which the student wants to do a job or in which the student's skill or efficiency is high its called GIM.


Why GIM only?


Often students choose the field according to the appearance of friends, advice of people or home situation which actually gives very bad results going forward, so the field should always be chosen keeping in mind their own skills and work skills.

Now the question is that how to really recognize our skills or work skills?


So for that, GIM (Graduate in multimedia) course has been prepared by consulting experts, industry experts and psychologists, in which the inner strength and skill of the student is identified and only after that a field is selected.
